Capstone Archive

The Challenge

Redesign the capstone experience for the iSchool - from a team's project page to the general navigation experience - with a goal to provide a more usable and dynamic way of showcasing each team's amazing work. With these changes, we want to facilitate a stronger connection between industry partners and associates of the iSchool.

The Outcome

A dynamic navigation design to allow users to explore projects and have an individual project page that shows a more holistic view of the capstone experience. Also, the site explicitly displays companies the iSchool is working with in order to promote those connections.

The Impact

We are reimagining each aspect of the capstone experience to make it better for future generations of instructors, sponsors, and students of the iSchool. Students have a customizable space to display their projects, reducing the redundant deliverables in our program. We also make it easier for industry partners to find projects that pertain to them in order to keep them interested in sponsorship.

Sponsored By: The University of Washington iSchool
